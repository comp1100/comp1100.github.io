# 1‑on‑1 Chat: Weeks 3-4 (covers Weeks 1–2)

**What this is (15 min):**  
A short, relaxed check‑in with your legend to see how you’re settling in, what you’ve learned so far, and how your team is going. It’s about helping you make progress.

**What this isn’t:**
- Not a test or grading discussion  
- Not conflict mediation  
- Not counselling

**It’s normal to feel…**  
A bit overwhelmed, unsure, or like “everyone else gets it.” You’re not behind—this is a common Week‑3 feeling.

---

## Structure (how we’ll use the 15 minutes)

- **0–5 min: General check‑in.** How are you going? Any early wins or sticking points?  
- **5–10 min: Course concepts.** Key ideas from the first two studios.  
- **10–15 min: Team & next steps.** How your team is working; agree one small goal.

---

## How to prepare

No preparation or study needed. Bring something to take notes. Skim the prompts below and think of **one example** for each—real situations beat theory.

---

## Topics to Think About (Weeks 1–2)

1. Growth Mindset vs Fixed Mindset
    - Where did you place yourself on the mindset spectrum?  
    - When did you catch a fixed‑mindset reaction (e.g., tough bug, group task), and how might you reframe it next time?

2. Team Dynamics & Awareness Circle / TimeGuessr
    - What different working styles did you notice?  
    - How did your team make decisions when opinions differed?

3. Team Values & Contract
    - Which **actionable** values did your team choose, and why did you back them?  
    - If someone isn’t pulling their weight, what does your contract say to do first?

---

## Action plan

Leave with **one clear, small goal** for the next fortnight (e.g., speak up once per meeting; read the next studio before class).
